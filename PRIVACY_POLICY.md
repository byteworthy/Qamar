## Introduction

Noor ("we," "our," or "us") is an Islamic reflection and personal growth mobile application that helps users navigate life's challenges through structured thinking grounded in Islamic spiritual principles. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with the utmost care and respect.

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Noor mobile application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

#### Account Information
- **Email Address:** Used for account creation and communication
- **Username:** Optional display name (you can remain anonymous)

#### Journal & Reflection Data

- **Personal Thoughts:** Your thoughts and reflections on daily challenges
- **Thought Patterns:** Identified patterns in your thinking
- **Reframed Perspectives:** AI-generated alternative viewpoints with Islamic guidance
- **Intentions (Niyyah):** Your personal intentions and goals
- **Practice Notes:** Notes on Islamic practices you're exploring
- **Emotional State:** Your emotional state and intensity levels

#### Islamic Content Interaction
- **Reading History:** Which Quran verses and Hadith you've read (optional tracking)
- **Practice Logs:** Which Islamic practices you've engaged with
- **Reflection Notes:** Your personal reflections on Islamic content

### Automatically Collected Information

#### Usage Data (Anonymized)
- **Features Used:** Which screens and features you access
- **Session Duration:** How long you use the app
- **Button Clicks:** Which actions you take (anonymized)
- **App Performance:** Load times, errors (for bug fixing)

#### Device Information
- **Device Type:** iPhone, iPad, Android device model
- **OS Version:** iOS or Android version
- **App Version:** Which version of Noor you're using
- **Device ID:** Anonymized identifier for analytics

#### Technical Data
- **Crash Reports:** Error logs when app crashes (no personal data)
- **Performance Metrics:** App speed and responsiveness data
- **API Usage:** Number of AI requests (for rate limiting)

## How We Store Your Information

### Client-Side Storage (Your Device)

#### Secure Storage
- **iOS:** Keychain (hardware-encrypted)
- **Android:** Keystore (hardware-encrypted)
- **Stored Securely:**
  - Journal entries
  - Session data
  - Push notification tokens
  - Billing information

#### Regular Storage (Less Sensitive)
- **AsyncStorage:** Only for non-sensitive data
- **Stored Plainly:**
  - App preferences (theme, notification settings)
  - Onboarding completion status
  - Last app version used

### Server-Side Storage (Our Servers)

#### Encrypted Data
- **AES-256-GCM Encryption:** Military-grade encryption
- **Unique IV:** Each entry has unique encryption vector
- **Encrypted Fields:**
  - Your thoughts
  - Reframes
  - Intentions

#### Unencrypted Data (For Functionality)
- **Distortion Types:** Identified cognitive distortions (for insights)
- **Timestamps:** When reflections were created
- **Emotional States:** Detected emotional states (for patterns)
- **Session IDs:** For linking related data

## Data Retention

### User-Controlled Deletion
- **Individual Entries:** Delete any reflection anytime
- **Account Deletion:** Delete entire account from settings
- **Export First:** Export your data before deletion (JSON format)

### Automatic Deletion
- **30-Day Policy:** Sessions older than 30 days automatically deleted
- **Configurable:** We may extend retention in future (with notice)
- **Backups:** Encrypted backups retained for 90 days (for recovery)

### After Account Deletion
- **Immediate:** Journal entries deleted from active database
- **30 Days:** Data fully purged from all systems
- **90 Days:** Backups deleted
- **Forever:** Anonymized analytics may be retained (no personal data)

## Data Sharing & Third Parties

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data
**Never. Ever.** Your mental health and spiritual journey data will never be sold, rented, or shared for marketing purposes.

### Third-Party Services We Use

#### 1. Anthropic (Claude AI)
- **Purpose:** Generate cognitive behavioral reframes and insights
- **Data Sent:** Your thoughts, distortions, emotional state
- **Data Retention:** Anthropic may cache for performance (30 days max)
- **Privacy Policy:** https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
- **Safety:** No training on your data, HIPAA-compliant infrastructure

#### 2. Sentry (Error Tracking)
- **Purpose:** Monitor crashes and errors
- **Data Sent:** Error messages, stack traces, device info
- **PII Scrubbing:** Automatic removal of personal information
- **Privacy Policy:** https://sentry.io/privacy/
- **Optional:** Can be disabled in settings

#### 3. Firebase (Analytics)
- **Purpose:** Understand app usage patterns
- **Data Sent:** Screen views, button clicks (anonymized)
- **No Personal Data:** No journal entries, thoughts, or reflections
- **Privacy Policy:** https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **Optional:** Can be disabled in settings

#### 4. Stripe (Payment Processing - Web Only)
- **Purpose:** Process subscription payments for web version
- **Data Sent:** Email, payment method (handled by Stripe)
- **No Card Storage:** We never see your payment details
- **Privacy Policy:** https://stripe.com/privacy
- **PCI Compliant:** Industry-standard payment security

#### 5. Apple / Google (In-App Purchases)
- **Purpose:** Process in-app subscriptions
- **Data Sent:** Apple/Google ID, purchase receipts
- **Handled Entirely:** By Apple/Google (we only verify receipts)
- **Privacy Policies:**
  - Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  - Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### We DO Share With:
- **Law Enforcement:** Only if legally required by valid court order
- **Safety Emergencies:** If we believe there's imminent danger to you or others
- **Business Transfer:** If Noor is acquired (with notice to you first)

## Security Measures

### How We Protect Your Data

#### Device-Level Security
- **Secure Storage:** iOS Keychain / Android Keystore
- **Biometric Auth:** Face ID, Touch ID, Fingerprint
- **Jailbreak Detection:** Warns about compromised devices
- **Screenshot Prevention:** Sensitive screens can't be captured

#### Network Security
- **HTTPS Only:** All communication encrypted (TLS 1.3)
- **Certificate Validation:** Server identity verified
- **Rate Limiting:** Prevents brute force attacks
- **HMAC Signing:** Tamper-proof session tokens

#### Server Security
- **AES-256-GCM:** Military-grade encryption at rest
- **Railway Hosting:** SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure
- **Regular Updates:** Security patches applied immediately
- **Minimal Access:** Only essential team members have access

#### Mobile App Security
- **Code Obfuscation:** Prevents reverse engineering (ProGuard/R8)
- **Root Detection:** Identifies compromised devices
- **Secure Coding:** TypeScript strict mode, no vulnerable patterns
