# Noor

Noor is a structured thinking and reflection app for Muslims whose minds feel noisy, circular, or spiritually conflicted. It helps users slow down their thoughts, reflect intentionally, and build clarity through guided prompts rooted in everyday reasoning and Islamic perspective.

## What Noor Is

- A personal reflection companion
- A structured thinking tool
- A private space for journaling and thought clarity

## What Noor Is Not

- Not therapy
- Not medical care
- Not diagnosis
- Not crisis support
- Not mental health treatment

## How It Works

Users complete short reflection sessions.

Each session guides the user through capturing a thought, examining it, reframing it, and setting an intention.

All data is stored locally unless otherwise configured.

## Privacy

User reflections are stored locally on device.

Users can delete all reflections at any time from the profile screen.

## Audience

Built for Muslims who want clarity, structure, and grounded reflection without clinical framing.
