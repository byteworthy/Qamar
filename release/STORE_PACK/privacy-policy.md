# Noor Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## What Noor Is (and Isn't)

Noor is an Islamic companion app that brings together Quran reading, Arabic language learning, daily reflections, hadith exploration, and prayer features for Muslims. It is NOT:
- Professional counseling services
- Religious authority or fatwa issuance
- Emergency support for urgent situations

For urgent situations, please reach out to trusted support or call 988 Lifeline.

## What We Collect

Noor collects the following information when you use the app:

- Entries and reflections you write
- Responses generated during your sessions
- Arabic learning progress (flashcard history, quiz results, streaks)
- Basic account information (email address if you create an account)
- Device type and operating system version for app compatibility

## What We Do Not Collect

- Your real name or physical address
- Location data
- Contacts or photos
- Health records
- Biometric data
- Third-party tracking identifiers

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

- Provide personalized reflections and Islamic guidance
- Generate personalized responses to support your learning and self-reflection
- Power Arabic learning features including flashcard scheduling and progress tracking
- Save your session history so you can review past entries
- Improve app performance and fix issues

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not build profiles about you. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## How Your Data Is Processed

Noor processes your entries to generate personalized reflective content, Arabic tutoring responses, pronunciation feedback, translation explanations, and answers to Islamic knowledge questions. Your entries are sent to our content generation service to create these personalized responses. This processing is essential to how the app works.

Your data is not used to train any external models. It is processed solely to generate your personalized responses and is not retained by our processing service beyond what is needed to fulfill your request.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained for 30 days, then automatically deleted. Data is stored securely on our servers (encrypted in transit and at rest).

## Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

## We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.

## Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact: support@byteworthy.com
