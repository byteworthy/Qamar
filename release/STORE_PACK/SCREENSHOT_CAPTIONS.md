## Caption Design Specs

### Typography
- Font: System default (SF Pro) or simple sans-serif (Inter, Helvetica, Arial)
- Weight: Regular or Medium (not Bold)
- Size: Large enough to read on mobile, but not dominating

### Colors
- Text: Dark gray (#2C3E50) or black (#000000)
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) with 80-90% opacity OR fully opaque white bar

### Layout
- Position: Top 25% or bottom 25% of screenshot
- Alignment: Center
- Padding: Adequate space around text (20-30px)
- Lines: Keep to 1-2 lines maximum per caption

### Tone Rules
- ✓ Calm, clear, simple
- ✓ Benefit-focused ("Get clarity" not "Uses AI to...")
- ✓ No medical claims
- ✓ No therapy claims
- ✗ No buzzwords ("revolutionary", "breakthrough")
- ✗ No hype ("amazing", "incredible")
- ✗ No technical jargon

## Fast Execution in Canva

1. **Create new design**
   - Custom size: 1290 x 2796 px
   
2. **Upload screenshot as background**
   - Drag simulator screenshot into canvas
   
3. **Add text box**
   - Position at top or bottom
   - Add white rectangle behind text (80% opacity)
   - Type caption
   
4. **Download**
   - File type: PNG
   - Quality: Highest
   
5. **Repeat for 3-5 screenshots**
