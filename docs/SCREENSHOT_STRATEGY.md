## Design Guidelines

### Text Overlay Design
- **Font:** Clean sans-serif (Poppins, Inter, SF Pro)
- **Size:** Title 36-42pt, Secondary 18-24pt
- **Colors:** White text on dark gradient overlay, or Islamic green/gold
- **Positioning:** Top third or bottom third (avoid center)
- **Readability:** Test at thumbnail size (App Store preview)

### Islamic Visual Elements
- **Patterns:** Geometric Islamic patterns (NOT religious symbols)
- **Colors:** Gold (#D4AF37), Islamic Green (#009688), Deep Blue (#1A237E)
- **Typography:** Modern + Islamic (avoid overly ornate fonts)
- **Balance:** Professional + spiritual (NOT overly religious)

### Do NOT Include
- ❌ Clinical imagery (therapist, doctor, stethoscope)
- ❌ Mental health terminology on screenshots
- ❌ Crisis hotline numbers (that signals mental health app)
- ❌ Medical symbols (cross, heart rate monitors)
