# Qamar App Preview Video Script (30 seconds)

## Purpose
App preview videos increase conversion rate by 20-30%.
Required: 15-30 seconds, no audio required (but recommended).
iOS accepts .mov or .mp4 (1080p, 30fps minimum).

## Shot List

**0-5s: Hero Opening**
- Screen: Quran reader showing Al-Fatiha
- Action: Tap tajweed toggle → Arabic letters slowly gain color (ghunnah = green, ikhfa = blue)
- Then: Tap play → first word highlights gold as audio plays
- Text overlay (lower third): "Read the Quran with tajweed & audio"

**6-10s: Prayer Times**
- Transition: Smooth swipe to prayer times tab
- Screen: Prayer times screen with today's prayers
- Action: Show qibla compass with needle pointing
- Text overlay: "Never miss prayer"

**11-15s: Hifz Memorization**
- Transition: Tap Learn tab
- Screen: Hifz dashboard with progress map
- Action: Tap a juz cell → enter recitation screen → verse text visible
- Text overlay: "Memorize with spaced repetition"

**16-20s: Study Plan**
- Transition: Back to Learn tab
- Screen: Study Plan with weekly calendar
- Action: Tap a daily task → checkmark animation
- Text overlay: "Personalized weekly plans"

**21-25s: Arabic Tutor**
- Transition: Tap Arabic Tutor card
- Screen: Chat interface with Arabic question and response
- Text overlay: "Learn Arabic with personalized coaching"

**26-30s: Closing**
- Quick montage: Quran → Prayer → Hifz → Learn
- Qamar logo + icon center frame (fade in)
- Text: "One app instead of five"
- Fade to black
- App Store/Google Play badge

## Audio
- Option A: Soft Quran recitation (Al-Fatiha, Alafasy) at 30% volume throughout
- Option B: Subtle Islamic-inspired background music
- No voiceover needed

## Production Notes
- Record on iPhone 15 Pro or Pixel 8 Pro for best quality
- Or use iOS Simulator with QuickTime screen recording
- Resolution: 1080x1920 minimum, 1290x2796 for iPhone 15 Pro Max
- Edit in: iMovie (free), Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve (free)
- Export: H.264 MP4, 30fps, 30 seconds max
- iOS App Preview: Upload as .mov or .mp4
- Google Play: Upload as YouTube link OR direct file

## Estimated Production Time
- Recording: 1-2 hours
- Editing: 2-4 hours
- Total: Half day
