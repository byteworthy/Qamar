### Full Description

Noor is the all-in-one Islamic companion designed for your daily spiritual life. Whether you are reading Quran, checking prayer times, learning Arabic, or reflecting on your day, Noor brings everything together in one beautifully crafted app.

QURAN READER
Complete Quran with all 114 surahs, English translations, and authentic audio recitation. Bookmark your place and continue reading anytime.

PRAYER TIMES & QIBLA
Accurate prayer times based on your GPS location with multiple calculation methods. Built-in Qibla compass uses device sensors for reliable direction finding. Set notifications so you never miss salah.

ARABIC LEARNING
Learn 356 vocabulary words with spaced-repetition flashcards powered by the FSRS algorithm. Track streaks and hear native pronunciation for every word.

HADITH LIBRARY
Browse 200+ authentic hadiths from the six most trusted collections (Kutub al-Sittah). Search by topic, bookmark favorites, and share with others.

ADHKAR & DUAS
Access 100+ authentic morning, evening, and prayer adhkar with built-in tap counters. Receive daily recommended selections to keep your dhikr consistent.

GUIDED REFLECTION
A structured journaling tool that helps you process thoughts through an Islamic lens. AI recognizes thought patterns and offers perspectives rooted in Quranic wisdom. Set niyyah (intentions) and track your spiritual growth.

PRIVACY & SECURITY
Your reflections are sacred. Noor protects them with biometric authentication, AES-256 encryption, and a strict no-tracking policy. No ads. No data selling. Ever.

PREMIUM OPTIONS
Noor is free to use with core features. Upgrade to Plus or Pro for unlimited AI reflections, advanced analytics, and premium content.

Download Noor today and bring consistency and depth to your daily spiritual practice.

## Privacy Policy Requirements Checklist

Before submission, verify that the published privacy policy covers:

- [ ] Data types collected (location, journal entries, usage analytics)
- [ ] Purpose of data collection for each type
- [ ] Third-party services used (Anthropic AI, RevenueCat, Sentry)
- [ ] Data retention period (30-day automatic deletion for reflections)
- [ ] User rights: access, export, and delete personal data
- [ ] Children's privacy (COPPA compliance — app is 4+ rated)
- [ ] Data encryption methods (AES-256-GCM at rest, HTTPS in transit)
- [ ] Biometric data handling (processed on-device only, never transmitted)
- [ ] Location data usage (prayer times and Qibla only, not stored server-side)
- [ ] AI data handling (Anthropic does not train on user data)
- [ ] Contact information for privacy inquiries
- [ ] GDPR compliance for EU users (right to erasure, data portability)
- [ ] CCPA compliance for California users
- [ ] Date of last policy update
