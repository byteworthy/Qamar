# Noor Disclaimers

Noor is a structured reflection practice for Muslims who want clarity before action.

## What Noor Is

- A guided reflection companion
- A tool for examining thoughts with intention
- Grounded in Islamic values of patience, trust, and accountability

## What Noor Is Not

- Not therapy
- Not medical care or advice
- Not religious authority

## AI Boundaries

- Noor uses AI to generate reflection guidance
- AI responses are not reviewed by humans before delivery
- AI can make mistakes
- Use your own judgment and discernment

## Privacy

Reflections are stored securely on our servers (encrypted). Data is retained for 30 days, then automatically deleted. You can delete your data at any time.

## Subscriptions

All subscriptions are processed by Apple or Google.
