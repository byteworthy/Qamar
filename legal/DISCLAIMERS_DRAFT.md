## 1. Mental Health Disclaimer

### NOT A SUBSTITUTE FOR PROFESSIONAL CARE

Noor CBT is a self-guided reflection tool that uses cognitive behavioral techniques within an Islamic framework. It is NOT:

- ❌ A substitute for professional mental health treatment
- ❌ Medical or psychological diagnosis
- ❌ Therapy or counseling
- ❌ Treatment for mental health conditions

**When to Seek Professional Help:**
- Persistent thoughts of self-harm or suicide
- Severe depression, anxiety, or other mental health symptoms
- Symptoms interfering with daily life, work, or relationships
- Trauma requiring specialized care
- Any situation needing clinical intervention

**If you are in crisis:**
- Call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) - available 24/7
- Text "HELLO" to 741741 (Crisis Text Line)
- Call 911 or go to nearest emergency room
- Contact a mental health professional immediately
