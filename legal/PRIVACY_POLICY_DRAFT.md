## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Noor CBT ("we," "our," "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

**Key Principles:**
- Local-first: Your reflections stay on your device
- Minimal data collection
- No behavioral tracking or profiling
- No account required
- No sale of personal data

## Information We Collect

### Reflection Content (Stored Locally Only)
- **What**: Your thought entries, emotional ratings, reflections
- **Where**: Stored only on your device
- **Not Transmitted**: Reflection content remains on your device and is not sent to our servers or cloud storage
- **Deletion**: Removed when you uninstall the App

### Data Sent for AI Processing
- **What**: When you use AI features, your current thought/reflection is sent to OpenAI for processing
- **Purpose**: To generate CBT insights and reframes
- **Retention**: Not stored by us; see OpenAI's data policies
- **Not Used for Training**: Your data is not used to train AI models

### Session Metadata (Minimal)
- **What**: Session duration, interaction count, safety events (crisis detection triggers)
- **Purpose**: Safety monitoring and app functionality
- **Retention**: 30 days, then automatically deleted
- **No Personal Identifiers**: Logged with hashed user IDs only

### Subscription Status
- **What**: Whether you have an active subscription (Free, Plus, or Premium)
- **Source**: Managed by Apple or Google
- **Purpose**: Feature access control
- **No Payment Info**: We never see or store your payment details

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Precise location
- ❌ Contacts or address book
- ❌ Device identifiers for tracking
- ❌ Advertising ID
- ❌ Social media data
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Email or account credentials (no login required)
- ❌ Biometric data
- ❌ Health data from other apps

## How We Use Information

### Safety Monitoring
- Detect crisis language to display resources (988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
- Monitor for scrupulosity patterns to adjust AI tone
- Track high-distress states for appropriate pacing

### App Functionality
- Provide CBT journey features
- Manage subscription tier access
- Generate AI-powered insights (for paid subscribers)
- Save reflections locally on your device

### No Marketing or Tracking
- We do not use your data for advertising
- We do not track you across apps or websites
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not profile you for behavioral targeting

## Data Sharing

### Third-Party AI Service (OpenAI)
- **What**: Current reflection text sent for AI processing
- **When**: Only when you actively use AI features
- **Purpose**: Generate CBT insights and reframes
- **Their Policy**: See OpenAI's privacy policy at https://openai.com/privacy
- **Not for Training**: Your data is not used to train AI models per our agreement

### App Store Platforms (Apple / Google)
- **What**: Subscription status
- **Purpose**: Billing and subscription management
- **Managed By**: Apple or Google, not us
- **Their Policies**: See Apple and Google privacy policies

### No Other Sharing
- We do not share data with advertisers
- We do not share data with data brokers
- We do not share data for marketing purposes
- We do not sell personal information

## Data Security

**Encryption:**
- Data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
- Reflections encrypted on your device (AES-256-GCM)

**Access Controls:**
- Minimal data access (only essential operations)
- No human review of your reflections

**Limitations:**
- No security system is perfect
- Device security is your responsibility
- If your device is compromised, local data may be at risk

## Data Retention

**Reflection Content:**
- Stored locally on your device indefinitely until you delete
- Not backed up to cloud
- Removed when App is uninstalled

**Session Metadata:**
- Retained for 30 days
- Automatically deleted after 30 days
- Used only for safety monitoring

**Subscription Data:**
- Managed by Apple or Google
- We only see active/inactive status

## Your Rights

### Access and Control
- **View**: See your reflections in the History screen
- **Delete**: Delete individual reflections or clear all history
- **Export**: Premium subscribers can export data as JSON
- **Uninstall**: Remove all local data by uninstalling the App

### No Account Deletion
- Since no account is created, there is no account to delete
- Uninstalling the App removes all your local data

### Data Portability
- Premium users can export reflections as JSON
- You own your reflection content
