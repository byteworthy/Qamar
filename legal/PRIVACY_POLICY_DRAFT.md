## What Noor Is (and Isn't)

Noor is a self-reflection journaling tool rooted in Islamic principles. It is NOT:
- A substitute for professional counseling or medical care
- Emergency support for crisis situations
- Licensed therapy or healthcare
- Medical advice or diagnosis

For professional support, please consult a licensed counselor, therapist, or healthcare provider.

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Noor ("we," "our," "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

**Key Principles:**
- Reflections are stored securely on our servers (encrypted in transit and at rest)
- Users control their data and can delete it at any time
- Data is retained for 30 days, then automatically deleted
- Minimal data collection
- No behavioral tracking or profiling
- No account required

## Information We Collect

### Reflection Content (Stored Locally Only)
- **What**: Your thought entries, reflections
- **Where**: Stored only on your device
- **Not Transmitted**: Reflection content remains on your device and is not sent to our servers for storage
- **Deletion**: Removed when you uninstall the App

### Data Sent for AI Processing
- **What**: When you use AI features, your current thought/reflection is sent for processing
- **Purpose**: To generate reflection guidance
- **Retention**: Not stored by us
- **Not Used for Training**: Your data is not used to train AI models

### Session Metadata (Minimal)
- **What**: Session duration, interaction count
- **Purpose**: App functionality
- **Retention**: 30 days, then automatically deleted
- **No Personal Identifiers**: Logged with hashed user IDs only

### Subscription Status
- **What**: Whether you have an active subscription (Free, Plus, or Premium)
- **Source**: Managed by Apple or Google
- **Purpose**: Feature access control
- **No Payment Info**: We never see or store your payment details

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Precise location
- ❌ Contacts or address book
- ❌ Device identifiers for tracking
- ❌ Advertising ID
- ❌ Social media data
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Email or account credentials (no login required)
- ❌ Biometric data
- ❌ Health data from other apps

## How We Use Information

### App Functionality
- Provide reflection features
- Manage subscription tier access
- Generate AI-powered guidance (for paid subscribers)
- Save reflections locally on your device

### No Marketing or Tracking
- We do not use your data for advertising
- We do not track you across apps or websites
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not profile you for behavioral targeting

## Data Sharing

### Third-Party AI Service
- **What**: Current reflection text sent for AI processing
- **When**: Only when you actively use AI features
- **Purpose**: Generate reflection guidance
- **Not for Training**: Your data is not used to train AI models per our agreement

### App Store Platforms (Apple / Google)
- **What**: Subscription status
- **Purpose**: Billing and subscription management
- **Managed By**: Apple or Google, not us

### No Other Sharing
- We do not share data with advertisers
- We do not share data with data brokers
- We do not share data for marketing purposes
- We do not sell personal information

## Data Security

**Encryption:**
- Data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
- Reflections encrypted on your device

**Access Controls:**
- Minimal data access (only essential operations)
- No human review of your reflections

## Data Retention

**Reflection Content:**
- Stored locally on your device indefinitely until you delete
- Not backed up to cloud
- Removed when App is uninstalled

**Session Metadata:**
- Retained for 30 days
- Automatically deleted after 30 days

**Subscription Data:**
- Managed by Apple or Google
- We only see active/inactive status

## Your Rights

### Access and Control
- **View**: See your reflections in the History screen
- **Delete**: Delete individual reflections or clear all history
- **Export**: Premium subscribers can export data as JSON
- **Uninstall**: Remove all local data by uninstalling the App

### No Account Deletion
- Since no account is created, there is no account to delete
- Uninstalling the App removes all your local data

### Data Portability
- Premium users can export reflections as JSON
- You own your reflection content

## Children's Privacy

Users aged 13-17 should use the App with parental guidance.
