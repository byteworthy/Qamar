# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 24, 2026
**Last Updated**: January 24, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Noor ("we," "our," "the App") handles your information. Noor is operated by ByteWorthy LLC, based in Texas, United States.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## What Noor Is (and Isn't)

Noor is a self-reflection journaling tool rooted in Islamic principles. It is **NOT**:
- A substitute for professional counseling or medical care
- A diagnostic tool for mental health conditions
- Emergency support for crisis situations
- Licensed therapy or healthcare

**For professional support, please consult a licensed counselor, therapist, or healthcare provider.**

**In case of emergency, contact 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline or call 911.**

## Key Principles

- ✅ Reflections are encrypted and stored securely
- ✅ You control your data and can delete it anytime
- ✅ Data is automatically deleted after 30 days
- ✅ Minimal data collection
- ✅ No behavioral tracking or profiling
- ✅ No account required for basic use

## Information We Collect

### 1. Reflection Content

**What we collect:**
- Your thought entries and reflections
- Reframes, practices, and intentions you create

**How we store it:**
- Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted at rest on our servers
- Stored for 30 days maximum

**How we use it:**
- To provide app functionality (save your history)
- To generate AI-powered guidance (when you use AI features)

**How long we keep it:**
- Automatically deleted after 30 days
- You can manually delete anytime from the app

### 2. AI Processing Data

**What we send:**
- Your current thought text when you use AI features
- Context needed to generate reflection guidance

**Purpose:**
- To generate personalized CBT-based reflection responses
- To provide Islamic-informed perspective

**AI Provider:**
- We use OpenAI's API to generate responses
- Your data is not used to train OpenAI's models (per our data processing agreement)
- Sent via encrypted connection

### 3. Session Metadata

**What we collect:**
- Session timestamps
- Number of reflections completed
- Subscription tier (Free, Plus, Premium)
- Device type (iOS or Android)

**Purpose:**
- App functionality and feature access
- Usage analytics (aggregated, not personal)

**Retention:**
- 30 days maximum, then automatically deleted

### 4. Subscription Information

**What we collect:**
- Subscription status (Free, Plus, or Premium)
- Subscription start/end dates

**Managed by:**
- Apple (for iOS users via App Store)
- Google (for Android users via Google Play)

**What we DO NOT see:**
- Your payment details (credit card, billing address)
- Purchase history beyond active subscription status

**Access Controls:**
- Minimal employee access (only for essential operations)

- Automated deletion after 30 days

### Access and Control
- **View**: See all your reflections in the History screen
