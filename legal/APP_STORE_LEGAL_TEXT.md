## SECTION 1: PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated: January 2026**

### What We Collect

Noor collects the following information when you use the app:

- Journal entries and reflections you write
- Responses generated during your sessions
- Basic account information (email address if you create an account)
- Device type and operating system version for app compatibility

### What We Do Not Collect

- Your real name or physical address
- Location data
- Contacts or photos
- Health records
- Biometric data
- Third-party tracking identifiers

### How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

- Provide personalized reflections and journaling prompts
- Generate AI-powered responses to support your self-reflection
- Save your session history so you can review past entries
- Improve app performance and fix issues

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not build profiles about you. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

### AI Processing

Noor uses artificial intelligence to generate reflective responses based on what you write. Your entries are processed by AI systems to create personalized content. This processing is essential to how the app works.

### Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. Inactive accounts and their associated data are automatically deleted after 12 months of no activity.

### Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

### We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.

### Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact: support@byteworthy.com

## SECTION 3: SAFETY AND CRISIS DISCLAIMER

### Important Safety Information

Noor is a self-reflection and journaling tool. It is not a replacement for therapy, counseling, or professional treatment.

### What Noor Is Not

- Noor is not a therapist or counselor
- Noor is not a crisis intervention service
- Noor is not a medical device
- Noor does not involve licensed clinicians

### Not for Emergencies

This app is not designed for use during personal crises or emergencies. If you are in crisis, please reach out to trained professionals immediately.

### United States Crisis Resources

If you are in the United States and experiencing a crisis:

- **988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline**: Call or text **988**
- **Crisis Text Line**: Text HOME to **741741**
- **Emergency Services**: Call **911**

### International Users

If you are outside the United States, please contact your local emergency services or crisis hotline. Noor does not provide emergency support in any country.

### Your Safety Matters

If you are struggling, please seek help from a qualified professional. Noor is here to support your daily reflection practice, not to replace the care of trained providers.
